SQIL:Saliency-aware Quantized Imitation Learning

Under Review at ICCV 2025

[Paper] [Code] [Video]

Abstract

SQIL is a quantization-aware imitation learning method that selectively emphasizes mission-critical states using a perturbation-based saliency signal. This enables low-bit policies to maintain performance in critical decision regions under extreme quantization.

Highlights

  • 4-bit policy with minimal accuracy drop
  • State importance via perturbation saliency
  • Robust generalization across simulation and real-world tasks
